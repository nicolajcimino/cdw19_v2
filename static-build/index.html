About Cincinnati Design Week

Our 8th Annual Cincinnati Design Week is a multiple-day celebration made up of workshops, panel discussions, speaking events, and of course, great connections. This year, we plan to connect with over 1,800 local professionals in design.

Cincinnati Design Week is proudly organized by a small team of volunteers on the AIGA Cincinnati board. If you're interested in supporting our thriving creative community, we would love to have your time and talents. Please consider joining our team.
