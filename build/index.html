About Cincinnati Design Week

We are proud to present the 8th annual Cincinnati Design Week! CDW19 is a week-long celebration made up of workshops, panel discussions, speaking events, and of course, great connections. Every year, we bring together over 1,800 designers and creatives to re-energize the creative spirit that drives us. We use these 5 days to come together, learn new techniques, build new connections, and find inspiration.

About AIGA Membership

Membership isn't required to attend events during Cincinnati Design Week, but it does give you the opportunity to get tickets at a discounted rate and other member-only benefits. Not yet a member? Sign up today.

Events are not quite live, but here is a preview of what our line-up looks like

We are focused on bringing you the highest quality programming and most rewarding experiences. You asked for it, and we listened! We’ve heard for years that our community wanted more quality time at events, to make better connections, speak with the presenters, and enjoy the spirit of the moment. With that in mind, each day of Design Week will feature 2 events that are a minimum of an hour and a half in length. In addition to bringing back some well-loved favorites, (we see you, Agency Crawl!) other events presented this week pair up with our most popular initiatives as well as some amazing area companies.
