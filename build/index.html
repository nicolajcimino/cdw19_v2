About CDW

AIGA Cincinnati is proud to present our eighth annual Cincinnati Design Week! CDW19 will be a week-long celebration full of workshops, panel discussions, speaking events, and of course, great connections. Every year, we bring together and re-energize the creative spirits of 1,800+ designers and creatives. Join us as we learn new techniques, expand networks, and find inspiration over five action-packed days.
